# Prompt bank direction seeds

Useful large buckets for the 550 image prompts:

- Product photography: headphones, shoes, beverage cans, desk gear, ceramics, watches, backpacks, cosmetics, tech accessories.
- Architecture and interiors: libraries, studios, compact apartments, museums, cafes, university labs, night markets, transit stations.
- Food and drink: ramen, bubble tea, bento, fruit desserts, coffee, tea, hotpot, street food, product-style food ads.
- Music and instruments: acoustic guitar, fingerstyle scene, tiny stage, recording desk, chord notebooks, rehearsal room.
- Taiwan / Asia-inspired urban scenes: rainy Taipei street, Hsinchu tech park evening, scooters, lantern alleys, campus corners.
- Fashion/lifestyle: Korean minimalist outfits, rainy commute, summer campus look, editorial streetwear, accessories.
- Nature/travel: misty mountains, coastlines, forests, lakeside cabins, sunrise trails, tropical rain, tea farms.
- Sci-fi: compact labs, lunar greenhouse, city transit, robots, clean spacecraft interiors, holographic desks.
- Fantasy: cliff cities, harbor markets, floating gardens, magical workshops, luminous forests.
- UI/app/game assets: icons, inventory items, environment tiles, isometric props, dashboard hero images.
- Educational visual scenes: mechanical concepts as visual metaphors, energy transition city, acoustic wave visualization.
- Poster/thumbnail concepts: blank text areas, bold composition, graphic layout, event mood, social cover format.

For videos, favor slow motion/camera movement: push-in, tracking shot, aerial pan, macro reveal, dolly shot, rain reflections, simple subject action.
